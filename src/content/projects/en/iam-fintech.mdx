## Context

A regulated fintech with sensitive financial data, where **permission drift** is not just a hygiene issue — it's a compliance risk. The previous process relied on manual access reviews and ad-hoc offboarding.

## What I shipped

- **Lifecycle automation** for IAM/AD/RBAC across 500+ users
- **Automated offboarding** — eliminating the gap between "person leaves" and "access revoked"
- **CIS-aligned hardening** of 3 critical Linux servers + GPO baseline for Windows
- **EDR (Bitdefender) + SIEM (Wazuh)** integration with Windows Event Logs for detection in a sensitive environment

## Why it mattered

Compliance audits stopped being a fire drill. Permission drift went from "hopefully someone catches it" to "the system shows you what changed."
